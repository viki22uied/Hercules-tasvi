# **App Name**: Hercules Finance AI

## Core Features:

- Income Intelligence & Forecasting: Analyzes historical income data and leverages generative AI as a tool to forecast weekly income, proactively identifying potential income dips based on user's work patterns and broader economic trends.
- Automated Cash Flow Orchestration: Dynamically manages and coordinates user's spending, saving, and bill payments by creating personalized, automated plans based on their unique income and expense patterns.
- Emotional Stress Sensing: Detects potential financial distress via sentiment analysis on user input and transaction descriptions, providing timely and relevant assistance or resources.
- Personalized Crisis Response: Offers customized, actionable plans for managing financial shortfalls, including identifying available resources and optimizing expenses, without causing panic or anxiety.
- Family Finance: Provides collaborative financial planning tools that allow families to share financial responsibilities, track shared expenses, and understand overall household finances transparently.
- Cultural Investment Education: Offers personalized investment guidance and educational content that considers user's cultural background and traditional festivals (e.g., Diwali in Mumbai), explaining the stock market and investment opportunities in relevant, familiar contexts. Example locations: Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata, Bangalore, and Hyderabad
- Multi-Language Support: Offers seamless translation of all app content across English, Hindi, and Marathi, allowing users to switch languages easily for a personalized experience.
- Scam & Fraud Simulation: Uses AI to simulate fake calls and scams to help users understand and identify fraudulent activities, enhancing their awareness and protection against financial fraud.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: White (#FFFFFF) to give a clean and modern look.
- Secondary color: Light Gray (#F7F7F7) for backgrounds and subtle contrast.
- Accent color: Blue (#1A73E8) for key actions, links, and highlights, providing a sense of trust and reliability.
- Text color: Dark Gray (#202124) for readability and a professional feel.
- Body font: 'Roboto' for a clean and readable user interface.
- Headline font: 'Roboto' (Bold) to create hierarchy and draw attention to important titles.
- Use simple, outlined icons to represent various financial concepts, promoting easy understanding and a minimalist visual appeal.
- Employ a clean and intuitive layout with ample spacing to minimize clutter and enhance ease of navigation.
- Subtle animations and transitions for user interactions to provide feedback and a smooth user experience.
